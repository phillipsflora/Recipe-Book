Hot and Spicy|Chilli Cheese Toast | 5 Min Snack Recipe preparation
with step by step photos and a quick video. Chilli cheese toast is
one of the easiest evening snacks. It can be quickly prepared in no
time. I have added garlic, green chilies, and chili flakes which makes
this snack more spice and yummy. I have used mozzarella cheese which
is soft fresh cheese prepared from Cow’s milk. It has a mild flavor
with a flexible and rubbery texture. I have used Tawa and kept the
stove in low flame to prevent the bread from burning before cheese melts.
You can use white bread, brown bread, multigrain bread, or whole wheat
bread.
